Campaign playbook (v1)

Live App Store (US): Goalz: Goal Countdown Widget

Budget starter: about $25/day total — Google ~$7, Apple Search Ads ~$10, Meta ~$8.

Download on the App Store Home Markdown version

Tracking link template

https://apps.apple.com/us/app/goalz-goal-countdown-widget/id6762053420?utm_source={source}&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=goalz_launch_v1&utm_content={creative}

Use google, meta, or apple_search for {source}.

Apple Search Ads

  • Open searchads.apple.com → App promotion → select Goalz (com.ziro.goalzdots.iphone).
  • Start US only; conservative CPT; ~$10/day.
  • Keywords to test: goal tracker, countdown widget, lock screen widget, single goal app.

Google Ads

  • Open ads.google.com → App promotion → iOS → paste App Store URL with UTMs.
  • ~$7/day; avoid thrashing bids during learning (3–7 days).

Meta Ads

  • Open Ads Manager → App / Sales objective per your signal setup.
  • ~$8/day; use 9:16 and 1:1 creative showing widgets + countdown.

Measurement

Blended ROAS is attributed revenue divided by total ad spend. Pull spend from each platform; revenue from App Store Connect (and refunds).
